Our Founders

Land Donors

Two compassionate sisters who made this mission possible

Founding Donor

Smt. Kumari Sakuntala Bai

With immense generosity and compassion, Smt. Kumari Sakuntala Bai donated her family land to establish a permanent home for elderly people in need. Her selfless act of kindness laid the foundation for this charitable mission.

"May this land become a sanctuary of peace and dignity for those who have no one."
Founding Donor

Smt. Satyabhama

Following her sister's noble example, Smt. Satyabhama joined in this sacred mission by contributing her share of family land. Her generosity reflects the true spirit of "Jeevakaarunyam Jeevithathiloode" — Charity through Life.

"True wealth is not what we keep, but what we give for the welfare of others."

The Sree Vidyadhiraja Charity stands on land donated by these two sisters. Their generosity reminds us that even a single act of kindness can transform countless lives. We remain forever grateful for their vision and compassion.

Our Inspiration

Chattambi Swamigal

Known as Sree Vidyadhiraja — The sage who taught compassion through wisdom

Chattambi Swamigal (1853–1924)

Chattambi Swamigal occupies a special place in the spiritual history of Kerala. Emerging at a time when society was weighed down by rigid customs and caste-based divisions, he stood as a powerful voice of inner freedom and spiritual clarity.

He was not only a saint who immersed himself in meditation and scriptural study, but also a brilliant thinker who questioned blind traditions and encouraged people to use reason in their search for truth. His philosophy was rooted in compassion, equality, and respect for all.

Through his writings and personal example, he opened a new understanding of spirituality — one that was accessible to ordinary people and expressed through kindness toward all beings.

"True knowledge softens the heart and leads to service."

— Chattambi Swamigal
Our Reason

Why This Charity Exists

In many communities, elderly people who have no family are left without shelter, care, or dignity. They spend their final years in loneliness and struggle.

We believe that every person who has lived a lifetime deserves peace, respect, and compassion in their later years. Our foundation exists to provide that — a home where they are not forgotten, but cherished.

The land for this home was donated by two sisters with generous hearts. Now we need your help to build the shelter, dining hall, and medical facilities that will turn this vision into reality.

What We Do

Our Activities & Services

Providing a secure, dignified, and nurturing environment for the elderly

🏠

Residential Care & Essential Needs

  • Safe and comfortable living environment
  • Nutritious home-style meals daily
  • Clothing, blankets, and hygiene kits
  • 24/7 security and housekeeping
🩺

Medical & Healthcare Services

  • Regular health check-ups (BP, sugar, temperature)
  • 24/7 bedside nursing care
  • Physiotherapy and geriatric care
  • Medicine distribution and emergency ambulance
🎨

Recreational & Social Activities

  • Yoga, meditation, and light exercises
  • Puzzles, chess, and book reading
  • Festival celebrations and cultural events
  • Bhajans, movie nights, and outings
💝

Emotional & Psychological Support

  • Daily companionship to combat loneliness
  • Mental health counseling
  • Intergenerational activities with children
  • Support for loss and depression
🤝

Empowerment & Support Programs

  • Light vocational training for seniors
  • Awareness about elder rights
  • Community outreach and Annadhanam
  • Dignified last rites for residents
🕊️

Specialized Care Programs

  • Dementia and palliative care
  • Doctor-on-call services
  • Spiritual and bhajan sessions
  • Temple visits and spiritual guidance
